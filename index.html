ABOUT US

We Leave A Delicious Memory For You

Since our modest beginnings in 2005 with a little space in Toronto’s stylish Yorkville locale, ‘KlassyCafe ‘s development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Indian-roused takeout food. In contrast to other Indian eateries, ‘KlassyCafe’ was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else.

Our menu offers something for everybody, from light passage of plates of mixed greens and little nibbles to the inconceivable T Bone steak.
